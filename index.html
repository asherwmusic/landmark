Questions

The honest answers

Can you guarantee my business will appear in ChatGPT results? +
No — and anyone who promises that is not being straight with you. AI engines aren't fully predictable. What we can guarantee is that we implement every known technique that increases citation probability, track your results monthly, and keep optimizing. Most clients see measurable improvement in citation frequency within 4–8 weeks.
How is this different from regular SEO? +
Traditional SEO gets you ranked in a list of links. GEO gets your business name woven into the answer itself. They share some technical foundations — site authority, content quality — but GEO requires additional work: Bing indexing, FAQ schema, conversational content structure, and presence on the specific platforms AI engines trust as sources. Most SEO agencies aren't doing this yet.
Does GEO replace my existing SEO? +
No — GEO and SEO are complementary. Traditional search still drives significantly more total traffic. But AI search traffic converts at dramatically higher rates, and it's growing fast. The businesses winning in 2026 and beyond will do both. We work alongside your existing SEO, not against it.
How do you measure results? +
Every month we run the same battery of buyer-intent queries across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. We track citation frequency (how often you appear), share-of-voice versus competitors, sentiment in how you're described, and — where trackable — AI referral traffic to your website. You get screenshots and a clear report, not vague promises.
How quickly will I see results? +
Foundation fixes take 1–2 weeks to implement. New content takes 4–8 weeks to be indexed and appear in AI responses. Most clients see their first citation improvements around the 6-week mark. The compounding effect — where more citations lead to more authority leads to more citations — builds over 3–6 months.
What's included in the free audit? +
We run 5 buyer-intent queries for your specific business and location across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews, show you exactly where you appear (or don't), identify which competitors are being cited instead, and give you 3 quick-win recommendations. No strings attached — it takes us about 20 minutes and costs you nothing.
